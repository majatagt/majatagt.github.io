Unit 2, Object-oriented Information Systems

Unit learning outcomes
On completion of this unit the student will be able to:
・Understand the core components of an information system and their importance.
・Evaluate a range of issues and appropriate solutions or mitigations.

This week will build on the knowledge gained in the first lecturecast,
by discussing the individual elements of an information system as well as their role in the larger system.
We will also look at the reasons why information systems can fail and any appropriate mitigations that can be implemented.


Seminar - (ERAM) System

This week's case study will focus on the En Route Automation Modernisation (ERAM) system and its failure.

Instructions

Read and take notes of
Scott, A. & Menn, J. (2014) Exclusive: Air traffic system failure caused by computer memory shortage. Reuters.
